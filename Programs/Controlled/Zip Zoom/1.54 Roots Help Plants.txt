 Roots Help Plants
Look at the seed in our room!
What is that growing from the seed?
There are a lot of roots growing from the seed.
What are roots?
Roots are part of a plant that helps get food for a plant to grow.
There are some roots that are food for us to eat.
Roots help plants to stay in place, too.
We need to take our seed and put it in the ground.
The roots will work to help the plant stay in the ground.
It will help the plant get food to grow tall like us!
