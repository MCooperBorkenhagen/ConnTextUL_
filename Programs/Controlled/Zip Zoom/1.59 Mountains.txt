 Mountains
Mountains are tall places on Earth.
Earth would be a flat place without mountains.
There are many mountains to see in far away places.
Mountains can be found everywhere on Earth.
Some mountains can be found alone or part of other mountains.
Mountains can also be found in the sea.
Some people like to climb mountains.
It can be hard work to climb up a mountain.
It can also be hard work to go down a mountain, too.
Some people think the best place to be is at the top of a mountain.
