Pat and Hap.

Hap is Pat's pet cat. Hap naps on a mat.
"You can keep the mat to nap on.
I'll be back, Hap," says Pat. "I have
some tasks to do."

Hap taps a mask. Hap pats a sack.

Hap bats Dad's pack of ten pens.

Look! Scat! Pat is back.
Hap sets off for the mat.

Hap naps. Pat pets his back.

"Hap, Hap," says Pat.
"You have no pep."
