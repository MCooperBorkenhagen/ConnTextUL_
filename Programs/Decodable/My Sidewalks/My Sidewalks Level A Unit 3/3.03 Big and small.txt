Big and small.
Josh is big.  
Josh is six.  
Beth is small. 
 Beth is just one.
Can Beth walk?  
A little bit.  
She still falls a lot.  
Josh helps her walk.
Can Beth talk?  
A little bit.  
Beth can call Josh. 
 Then Josh grins at her.
Can Beth use her blocks? 
 A little bit.
Her blocks fall. 
 Josh helps her use them.
Can Beth swim? 
 Not now.
Can Josh swim? 
 Yes!  
Josh can swim like a fish.
Will Beth get big? 
 Yes! 
 Then Beth will walk and talk.  
Beth will use her blocks. 
 Beth will swim with Josh.
