The Pup and the Bug.

A big bug sat on
the end of a log.
The pup ran to get the bug.

The pup fell in the mud!

The pup got mud on the rug.

Mom said to get the pup in the tub!

A big bug sat on a cup in the tub.
The pup got up to get the bug.

The bug fell in the tub!

The bug and the pup had fun in the tub.
Mom sent Diz to get a mop.
