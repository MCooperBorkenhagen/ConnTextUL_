Chad's Problem.
Chad sits in bed on his soft quilt.
Chad has a big test with lots of math problems.
Chad wants to do well.
Chad will sit in bed and drill his math problems.
Chad drills lots of math problems.
Chad gets up to get a drink of water.
Chad brings the water in a glass.
The cat trips Chad.
The glass of water spills on his quilt.
Squish, squelch. The quilt is wet.
Chad cannot sit on the wet quilt!
Can Chad fix that problem?
Chad hangs the quilt in the bathtub to drip.
Chad thinks, "I can fix problems well. Next, I will fix math problems!"
