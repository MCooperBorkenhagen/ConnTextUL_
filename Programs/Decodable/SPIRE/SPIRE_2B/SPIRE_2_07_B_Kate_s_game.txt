Kate's Game.
It was five to nine, and Kate was still in bed."Wake up, Kate.  It is late," said Dad.Kate did rush to dress."I must get to the game on time!" said Kate. "I hope I am not late!"
Dad and Kate rode bikes to the game. Kate was not late.Kate will pitch.Kate did pitch the ball to June.Whack! June hit the ball.
Up went the ball. It went up fast.Will Kate catch the ball? "Run, Kate," Steve did yell. "Catch it!"Will June make it to home plate?Kate did catch the ball!June did not make it to home plate this time.Kate's side got six runs. Kate's side did win.
"Thanks, Dad," said Kate."You got us to the game on time.And you got to the ball on time!" said Dad.
