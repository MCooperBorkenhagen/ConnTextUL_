Lunch with the Gang.
Lunch with the gang is fun!
Nat said, "What can I do, Dad?"
"You can get the chips," said Dad.
"What can I do?" said Ming.
"You can bring the punch," said Dad.
Nat did bring the chips. Ming did bring the punch.
"What can I do?" Said Seth.
"You can bring the buns," said Dad.
"I will bring the hot dogs," Dad said. "Who has the tongs?"
Seth had a hot dog. Ming had a hot dog. Nat had a hot dog. Dad had a hot dog.
Lunch with the gang is fun!
