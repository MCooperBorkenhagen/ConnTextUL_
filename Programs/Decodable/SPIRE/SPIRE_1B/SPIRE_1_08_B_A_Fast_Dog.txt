A Fast Dog.
Rex is Dan's dog. Rex is a whippet. A whippet is a fast dog.
Min is Pam's dog. Min is a pug. A pug is a fat dog. 

What will Rex and Min do? Rex will run with Min.
What will Dan and Pam do? Dan will lunch with Pam.
Rex and Min run on the path. Whiz! The dogs go fast.
Which dog will win? Rex runs fast. Rex wins.
Then Min and Rex sit in the mud. Min is wet. Rex is hot.
The dogs get a bath!
