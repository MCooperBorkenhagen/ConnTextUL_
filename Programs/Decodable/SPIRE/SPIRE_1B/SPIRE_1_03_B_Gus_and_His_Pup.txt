Gus and His Pup.
Gus got a pup.
Gus and his pup had fun in the sun.
Gus sat and had jam on a bun. His pup dug in the mud.
Jam got on Gus. Mud got on his pup.
Gus got a tub and a rag. Gus got the jam off.
Gus did rub the mud off the pup.
Gus and his pup did run and run.
Gus and his pup did nap at last.
