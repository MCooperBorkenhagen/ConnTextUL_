The Monkey Bridge.

"Make me a bridge!"
roared the lion.

Number one monkey
went over the river.

Number two monkey
went over the river.

Number three monkey
went over.

Number four and
number five went over.

Five monkeys 
over the river!

"Here is the bridge,"
the monkeys said to the lion.

The lion went
over the bridge.

Ah - ah - ah - ah - 

choo - oo - oo!

And the lion went
into the river.
