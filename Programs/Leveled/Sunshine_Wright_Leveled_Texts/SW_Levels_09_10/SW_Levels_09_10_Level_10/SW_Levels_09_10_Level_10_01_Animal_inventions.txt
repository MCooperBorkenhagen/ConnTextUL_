Animal Inventions.

A sugar glider glides through the air.

A hang glider does, too!

A fish breathes under water.

A diver does, too!

A spider lets itself down on a line.

A mountain climber does, too!

A frog kicks through the water
with webbed feet.

A swimmer does, too!

A crocodile looks around
as it hides under water.

A submarine does, too!
A submarine hides under water.
It uses a periscope to see.

An elephant pushes things with its trunk.
A bulldozer does, too!

A giraffe reaches high places.

A crane does, too!
