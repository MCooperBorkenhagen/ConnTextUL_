The Cow in the Hole.

The cow was in the hole.
"We will get you out,"
said the farmers.

They pushed and pushed.
But the cow did not get out.

"We will get you out,"
said the police officers.
They pushed and pushed.
But the cow did not get out.

"We will get you out,"
said the firefighters.
They pushed and pushed.
But the cow did not get out.

"We will get you out,"
said the children.
They got some hay
for the cow.

And the children
got the cow out of the hole.

"Hooray!" said the people.
