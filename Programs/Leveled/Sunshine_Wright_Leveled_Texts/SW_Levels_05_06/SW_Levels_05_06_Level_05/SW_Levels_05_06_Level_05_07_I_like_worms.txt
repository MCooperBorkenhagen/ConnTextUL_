I Like Worms!

I like big worms,
fat worms,
pink worms,
purple worms.

I like worms for lunch
and worms for tea.

I like little worms,
skinny worms,
red worms,
orange worms.

Worms, worms, worms
are good for me!

I like worms in my soup.
I like worms in my pies.

I like worms on my pizza.
I like worms and fries.

I like worms in a bowl.
I like worms in a jug.
I like worms in a jar.
I like worms in a mug.
I like worms!
