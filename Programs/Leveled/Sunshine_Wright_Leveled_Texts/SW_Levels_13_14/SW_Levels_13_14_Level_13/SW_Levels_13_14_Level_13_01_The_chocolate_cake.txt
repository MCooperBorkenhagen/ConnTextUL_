The Chocolate Cake.

"I'll make a chocolate cake,"
said Greg.

He made a big, big chocolate cake
and put it on the table.

Greg went to get Mom.

Dad saw
the chocolate cake on the table.
"I like chocolate cake," he said.
And he ate some.

Grandad and Grandma saw
the chocolate cake on the table.
"We like chocolate cake," they said.
And they ate some.

The birds saw
the chocolate cake on the table.
"We like chocolate cake," they said.
And they ate some.

Greg and Mom came into the kitchen.
They saw crumb on the table.
"Who ate my chocolate cake?"
said Greg.
"The birds!" said Dad.
