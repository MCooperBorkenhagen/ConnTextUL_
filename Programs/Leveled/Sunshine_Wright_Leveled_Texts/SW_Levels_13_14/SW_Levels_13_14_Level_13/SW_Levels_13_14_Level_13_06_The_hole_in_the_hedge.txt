The Hole in the Hedge.

A boy went down the road.
He saw a hole in the hedge.

He looked through the hole,
and he laughed and laughed
and laughed.

A girl went down the road.
"What are you laughing at?"
she said.
"Come and look!" said the boy.

The girl
looked through the hole in the hedge.
She laughed and laughed and laughed.

A woman went down the road.
"What are you laughing at?"
she said.
"Come and look!" said the girl.

The woman
looked through the hole in the hedge.
She laughed and laughed and laughed.


A police officer went down the road.
"What are you laughing at?"
he said.
"Come and look!" said the boy,
the girl, and the woman.

The police officer
looked through the hole in the hedge.

He saw elephants dancing
and giraffes scratching.

He saw zebras skating
and monkeys juggling.

He saw crocodiles flying
and kangaroos tapping.

He saw hippopotamuses hopping
and pelicans jumping.

The police officer
laughed and laughed and laughed.

The animals saw a hole in the hedge.

The animals looked through the hole
in the hedge, and they laughed
and laughed and laughed.
