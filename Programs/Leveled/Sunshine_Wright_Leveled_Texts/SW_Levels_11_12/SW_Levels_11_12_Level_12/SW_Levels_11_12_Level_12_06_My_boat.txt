My Boat.

I had a little boat, and it would not float, so I patched it up with banana skin.  

Did my boat float?

NO!

It sank to the bottom of the sea.

I had a little boat, and it would not float, so I patched it up with bubble gum.  

Did my boat float?

NO!

It sank to the bottom of the sea.

I had a little boat, and it would not float, so I patched it up with my brother's thumb.

Did my boat float?

NO!

It sank to the bottom of the sea.

I had a little boat, and it would not float, so I went with my brother to get another.

We got a new one, a blue one, a big-enough-for-two one ...

and we sailed across the sea.
